After looking at multiple non-profit organizations that needed help with their sites, we found the Junior Chefs of America. Their website has some great sections that are visibly pleasing. However, considering the usability and accessibility of the website, we found that there was room for improvement. In order to help users navigate the Junior Chefs of America, we wanted to redesign their site. The Junior Chefs of America is a nonprofit organization whose goal is to help children and adults through cooking. They offer professional training, and introductory classes to help keep kids engaged. They help their community through different initiatives.
